Here Are Some Reasons We Have Food Insecurity

Uncontrolled dumping and burning of garbage affect soil health, the air we breathe and causes climate change

Heaps of waste pollute water sources and farmlands,making the land unable to produce crops and the environment unable to support any plants

Wars also lead to displacement of people into overcrowded camps with limited supplies and this makes food insecurity imminent

Poor farming methods practiced by desparate farmers and rich pricks looking to pull a profit worsen the food situation

Climate change makes it harder for farmers to grow crops, worsening food insecurity

With dry lands and limited sources of water for irrigation, well food insecurity is inevitable
